Welcome To Our Company

Inovium makes on-demand business process automation products and services based on a unique, robust, and highly flexible financial services platform. Our products automate and simplify a number of complex manual business processes such as “procurement” and “invoicing and payments”, facilitating the users’ transition from paper to electronic processes and delivering overwhelming economic benefits. Inovium’s solutions are aimed at small to medium size businesses, municipalities and educational institutions, and are available for clients’ use under a Software-as-a-Service business model

Our clients enjoy:

• Exceptionally high ROI
• Operational streamlining
• More efficient usage of labor
• Getting paid faster
• Ability to invoice and receive payments electronically
• Secured transactions with 256-bit encryption codes
• Improved customer satisfaction and retention
• Absence of need to purchase or install software
• System flexibility and ability to support their growth
• Integration with 99% of major accounting systems
• “Financial grade” systems, developed by CPAs
